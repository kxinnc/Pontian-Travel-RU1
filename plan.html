Discover Your Unfold Beauty In Pontian

Stroll through lush mangroves, breathe in crisp sea air, and savor local delights rich in tradition. In Pontian, every moment invites you to slow down, embrace nature, and enjoy life's simple joys.

Don't Forget These Essentials!

Enjoy hotpot with fresh ingredients, rich broths, and stunning coastal views.

  • Sun Protection

    • Pack sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses—Pontian's outdoor spots can get sunny!

  • Comfortable Footwear

    • Whether exploring nature or walking around town, good shoes are a must.

  • Bug Repellent

    • Essential for visiting nature spots like Tanjung Piai and the bee farm.

Perfect One-Day Pontian Itinerary

Make the most of your day in Pontian with this carefully planned itinerary—featuring nature, culture, and delicious local food.

8:30 AM - 9:30 PM | Kickstart Your Day at Kopi Tenggek

Begin with a strong, traditional coffee at Kopi Tenggek, where coffee is brewed in a unique stacked pot system. Enjoy the rich aroma and a classic Malaysian breakfast to fuel your journey.

Google Map

09:45 PM - 11:30 PM | Explore Tanjung Piai National Park

Walk through lush mangrove forests, spot wildlife, and stand at the southernmost tip of mainland Asia. The coastal breeze and scenic views make it a perfect morning adventure.

Google Map

12:00 PM - 01:30 PM | Cool Down & Refresh at Ice House

Beat the heat with Pontian's famous jellyfish shaved ice at Ice House. This unique dessert, paired with crispy prawn crackers and fruit rojak, is the perfect midday treat.

Google Map

01:45 PM - 2:30 PM | Discover Pulau Kukup National Park

Take a boat ride to one of the world's largest uninhabited mangrove islands. Stroll along the boardwalk, climb the observation tower, and immerse yourself in this tranquil wetland ecosystem.

Google Map

03:00 PM - 5:00 PM | Uncle Tan Stingless Bee Farm

Step into a world of natural wonder and learn about the fascinating stingless bees of Johor. Uncle Tan's farm offers a unique opportunity to witness these gentle creatures at work, taste their rare and delicious honey, and understand their vital role in our ecosystem.

Google Map

05:45 PM - 06:30 PM | Hanada House

End the day with a cozy Japanese meal at Hanada House, where you can enjoy ramen, chicken cutlet rice, and tempura prawn udon in a warm, homely setting.
