A Foodie's Guide to Pontian's Best

Pontian's culinary scene is a delightful mix of tradition and innovation. From rich, aromatic coffee to comforting noodle dishes and refreshing local desserts, every bite carries a piece of the town's heritage.

Satisfying Meals to Fuel Your Journey

  • The Taste of Nostalgia

    • Kopitiam Jong Suan Hiang

    Step into a nostalgic coffee shop where every sip and bite bring back the taste of the past, with rich, aromatic coffee and crispy kaya toast.

    Charcoal Toasted Bread - Crispy on the outside, soft inside, perfectly paired with homemade kaya.

    Classic Kopitiam Coffee - Rich, aromatic, and brewed the traditional way for that authentic taste.

    Operation Time: Everyday except for Tuesday, 7:00 to 12:30

    Find on Google Map

  • A Cozy Taste of Japan in Pontian

    • Hanada House

    A charming neighborhood eatery serving comforting ramen, crispy cutlets, and tempura prawn dishes at wallet-friendly prices.

    Authentic Ramen - Rich, flavorful broth with perfectly cooked noodles.

    Warm & Inviting Ambiance - A cozy neighborhood spot for a comforting dining experience.

    Operation Time: Everyday except for Thursday, 11:30am to 3:30pm and 5:30pm-9:00pm

    Find on Google map

    Sweet & Refreshing Treats to Cool You Down

  • A Cool & Refreshing Treat

    • Yu Yu Ice (Shaved Ice)

    A classic dessert shop serving finely shaved ice with grape, red bean, corn, and sour plum toppings—perfect for cooling off on a sunny day.

    Signature Grape Ice - A sweet and fruity favorite with a vibrant purple hue.

    Classic Red Bean & Corn - A delightful mix of texture and taste.

    Operation Time: Everyday except for Tuesday, 10:00am to 4:00pm

    Find on Google Map

  • Cool Down with a Unique Treat

    • Summerly Ice House

    A one-of-a-kind dessert spot featuring refreshing jellyfish ice, crispy shrimp crackers, and flavorful rojak—perfect for adventurous foodies.

    Jellyfish Ice Dessert - A one-of-a-kind creation that surprises and refreshes.

    Flavorful Rojak - A mix of fresh fruits and a bold, tangy sauce for the perfect sweet-savory balance.

    Operation Time: Everyday except for Wednesday, 11:00am to 5:30pm

    Find on Google Map
