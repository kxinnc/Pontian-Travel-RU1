Immerse Yourself in Pontian's Breathtaking Nature

Step into a world of untouched beauty—where mangroves thrive, islands remain untouched, and lush farms tell stories of nature's abundance. Breathe in fresh air, listen to whispers of forest, and experience the heart of Pontian's great outdoors.

Discover the Heart of Pontian's Nature

  • A Floating Ecosystem

    • Taman Negara Pulau Kukup

    Step into a world of tranquility at Pulau Kukup, one of the largest uninhabited mangrove islands in the world. Stroll along wooden boardwalks, spot migratory birds, and witness an ecosystem thriving in perfect harmony.

    Floating Paradise - One of the world's largest uninhabited mangrove islands.

    Admission: Citizens Adult Rm5, Child / Senior Rm3, Non Citizens Adult Rm20, Child / Senior Rm10

    Operation Time: Everyday, 9:00am to 5:00pm

    Start Exploring

    Official Website

  • The Southernmost Tip of Asia

    • Taman Negara Johor Tanjung Piai

    Stand at the edge of the continent where the land meets the sea. Tanjung Piai is home to sprawling mangroves, rich biodiversity, and the symbolic globe monument marking Asia's southernmost point.

    Scenic Boardwalks - Walk above serene waters on a scenic pathway, surrounded by breathtaking coastal views and the soothing sounds.

    Admission: Citizens Adult Rm5, Child / Senior Rm3, Non Citizens Adult Rm20, Child / Senior Rm10

    Operation Time: Everyday, 9:00am to 5:00pm

    Start Exploring

    Nature's Bounty: From Hive to Harvest

  • A Buzzing Wonder

    • Uncle Tan Orchard Stingless Bee Farm

    Discover the fascinating world of stingless bees and their precious, nutrient-rich honey.

    Meet the Stingless Bees: Unlike regular honeybees, these tiny pollinators are harmless yet hardworking.

    Hands-On Activities - Try honey tasting, learn hive maintenance, and experience honey harvesting firsthand.

    Operation Time: Everyday, 9:00am to 5:00pm.

  • A Golden Harvest

    • NICTAR Pineapple Park

    Walk through endless fields of pineapple plants and uncover the secrets behind this tropical favorite.

    From Farm to Table - Experience the journey of fresh pineapples, from lush plantations to delicious homemade products.

    Admission: RM10 per person.

    Operation Time: Everyday, 9:00am to 5:00pm.
